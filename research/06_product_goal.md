# Product Goal

## Long-Term Vision
Create a flood-risk intelligence platform for Tasmania.

## Potential Users
- Insurance companies
- Emergency management agencies
- Local councils
- Infrastructure managers
- Researchers

## Proposed Capabilities
- Flood extent detection
- Flood susceptibility prediction
- Risk scoring
- Historical event comparison
- Automated updates
- Interactive dashboard

## Commercial Concept
Provide continuously updated flood-risk information that supports:
- Risk assessment
- Asset exposure analysis
- Insurance pricing support
- Disaster preparedness

## Future Expansion
- Tasmania-wide coverage
- Australia-wide coverage
- Multi-hazard capability (flood, bushfire, landslide)
