# Research Questions

## Primary Research Question
How accurately can Sentinel-1 SAR imagery detect and map the May 2018 Hobart flood, and how can this workflow be extended into a continuous flood-risk monitoring system for Tasmania?

## Secondary Questions
- What flood extent can be identified from SAR backscatter changes?
- How well do available Tasmania flood datasets support validation?
- What are the major uncertainty sources?
- Which environmental variables should be integrated in future flood-risk prediction models?
- How can the workflow support insurance-sector decision making?

## Scope
Initial focus: Hobart May 2018 flood.
Future focus: Tasmania-wide flood intelligence system.
