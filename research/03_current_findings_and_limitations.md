# Current Findings and Limitations

## What Appears Correct
- Sentinel-1 SAR is suitable for flood mapping.
- Google Earth Engine can support the workflow.
- Hobart 2018 flood can be investigated using Sentinel-1 data.
- Available Tasmania datasets provide useful contextual information.

## Current Problems
- Flood validation data quality remains uncertain.
- Some assets are not directly usable as raster flood extent layers.
- Certain datasets mainly represent river or water-level information.
- Direct flood-depth observations are limited.

## Risks
- Validation may not represent true flood extent.
- Water-level data may not be equivalent to flood extent data.
- Accuracy metrics may be affected by reference data quality.

## Knowledge Gaps
- Best validation dataset still needs confirmation.
- Additional government and emergency datasets should be investigated.
